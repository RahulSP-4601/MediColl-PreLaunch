# QB24 Waitlist Campaign

## Campaign Goal
Drive 200-500 qualified clinics and hospitals to join the QB24 early access waitlist before mid-May launch.

## Email Strategy

### Email 1: Initial Outreach
**Subject:** Never miss a patient call again

**Key Points:**
- Personal introduction from CEO Rahul Panchal
- Clear value proposition: 24/7 AI voice receptionist
- Problem statement: missed calls, busy lines, after-hours unavailability
- 5 key benefits clearly listed
- Launch timeline: mid-May / early June
- Call-to-action: Join waitlist
- Note about prioritization by waitlist order

**Design:**
- Gradient header (purple to violet)
- Clean, modern layout
- Visual icons and checkmarks
- Prominent CTA button
- Professional signature

---

### Email 2: Follow-up (3 days later)
**Subject:** Just checking — worth exploring for your clinic?

**Key Points:**
- Quick follow-up reminder
- Simplified value proposition
- Same CTA: Join early access
- Request for feedback if not interested
- Helps improve product based on clinic input

**Design:**
- Similar styling to Email 1 for brand consistency
- Shorter, more concise
- Feedback request box highlighted
- Professional but friendly tone

---

## Email Formats

Both HTML and plain text versions created for:
- ✅ Better deliverability (multipart MIME)
- ✅ Compatibility with all email clients
- ✅ Reduced spam score

**Files:**
- `config/email_templates/initial_email.html`
- `config/email_templates/initial_email.txt`
- `config/email_templates/follow_up_1.html`
- `config/email_templates/follow_up_1.txt`

---

## Target Metrics

### First 30 Days:
- 10,000 emails sent
- >20% open rate
- >5% click rate (500 clicks)
- >2% waitlist signups (200 signups)
- >1% reply rate (100 replies)

### Weekly Progression:
- **Week 1:** 350 emails → 10 signups
- **Week 2:** 700 emails → 30 signups
- **Week 3:** 1,400 emails → 60 signups
- **Week 4:** 2,800 emails → 100 total signups
